School Supplies

School supplies are the various tools and items that students use for their studies, such as notebooks, pens, pencils, erasers, binders, folders, and textbooks. These supplies can range from basic writing instruments to more specific items like calculators, rulers, glue, and even digital devices like tablets or laptops, depending on the grade level or course of study.

Medical Assistance

A medical assistant is a healthcare professional who performs both administrative and clinical tasks to support doctors, nurses, and other medical staff in medical offices, hospitals, and clinics. Their duties include scheduling appointments, managing medical records, taking vital signs, assisting with exams, and explaining medications, all under the supervision of a physician or other healthcare professional.
